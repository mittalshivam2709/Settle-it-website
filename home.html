About Us

SETTLE IT is a site for one of the most common problems today - splitting money.
In the age of digital payment, it has become more and more common for transactions,
especially between groups of people, to occur online. Which begs the question -
how will that money be settled?

That is where SETTLE IT comes in.


SETTLE IT offers ease of convenience and a smooth platform through which adults
can easily perform even the most complicated transactions without worry.
